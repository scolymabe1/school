Solutions For The Doubts

Frequently Asked Questions

Find all the essential information you need in our FAQ section, designed to address the most frequently asked questions and help you make informed decisions for your child's education.

What are the school hours at Little Learners Academy?

Our school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday to Friday. We also offer extended care options for early drop-off and late pick-up.

Is there a uniform policy for students?

Yes, students follow a simple and comfortable uniform policy. The uniform helps create a neat, equal, and focused school environment.

What extracurricular activities are available for students?

We offer art, music, storytelling, outdoor games, simple sports, creative workshops, and group activities that support social and emotional growth.

What age groups do you accept?

We accept young learners in early childhood age groups and place them into classes based on their age, development level, and learning needs.

How do you handle food allergies and dietary restrictions?

We work closely with parents to understand each child's allergies and dietary needs. Meals and snacks are carefully monitored by our staff.

What is the teacher-to-student ratio at Little Learners Academy?

We maintain small class sizes so teachers can give children personal attention, support, and guidance throughout the school day.

How do you handle discipline and behavior management?

We use positive guidance, clear routines, gentle correction, and age-appropriate conversations to help children understand good behavior.

How do I apply for admission to Little Learners Academy?

You can apply by contacting our admissions team, visiting the school, or filling out the application form. Our team will guide you through the next steps.
